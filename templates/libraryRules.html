Library Book Rental Rules

  • 📚 Books can be borrowed for a maximum of 14 days.
  • 🔄 Renewal is allowed once if no one else has reserved the book.
  • ⏰ A late fee of ₹5 per day is charged for overdue books.
  • 📅 Members must return or renew books before the due date.
  • 🧾 Lost or damaged books must be reported and compensated for.
  • 💳 A valid library membership is required to rent books.
  • 📖 A maximum of 3 books can be borrowed at a time.
  • 📲 Notifications will be sent via email or SMS before due dates.
  • 🚫 Silence and proper conduct must be maintained in the library.
